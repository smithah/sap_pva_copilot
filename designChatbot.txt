Design/Approach of a chatbot:
1) Identify conversation scenarios between a user and a bot.
2)
a) For each line identify intents( which is Topics in PVA)
b) Intents can be identified based on multiple ways a sentence can be framed.
Example : Hi, Hello, Hey, Yo...( These are trigger phrases for Topic, intent is Greeting here)
3) Once intent identified, extract the necessary entity from the conversation.
4) Based on the identified  entity and intent, the corresponding backend action (PA flows - in our case KB of HR policy) to be called to fetch the answer.

So we need to design/frame steps 1,2 and 3 above(at PVA end) - calling PA flow with the structured question to KB must be straightforward.

Bot composer comes into picture when we need multi-turn by providing clickable options.

Bot Framework Skills are re-usable conversational skill ( other chatbots on specific skills) to be called in this chatbot.
